Meet Your Experts

They've seen it all and are eager to help

Olufunke Afolabi-Brown, M.D.

The BabyCenter 3-stage sleep solution

Dr. Brown is a board certified pediatric pulmonary and sleep medicine physician. She is the founder of Restful Sleep MD, where she helps families prioritize and improve their sleep. She lives in Philadelphia with her husband and two children.

Michelle Tiflinsky, M.S., OTR/L

Meeting physical milestones through play

Michelle has been a pediatric occupational therapist for more than a decade and is the founder of Purposeful Play NYC in New York City. Her two young daughters taught her how challenging it can be to follow occupational therapy best practices at home and to prevent developmental delays, which is why she's so passionate about educating parents on purposeful movement and play.

C. Anthoney Lim, M.D., M.S.

Infant CPR and first aid

Dr. Lim is the director of pediatric emergency medicine for the Mount Sinai Health System. In addition to seeing patients, he works to ensure consistent and quality emergency care for children across Mount Sinai's eight hospitals. He pursued a career in pediatric medicine to help parents, not just children.
